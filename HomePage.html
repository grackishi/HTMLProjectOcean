Different Ocean Layers

There are 4 main ocean layers. Each layer contains its own variety of different sea creatures and interactions between them. The 4 main layers are the Epipelagic Zone, the Mesopelagic Zone, the Bathypelagic Zone, and the Abyssopelagic Zone. There is also the Hadal Zone which is the deepest part of the ocean. These trenches and very deep sections of the ocean remain largely unexplored due to the immense pressures that exist at those depths. On this site, we will be looking at interactions between the inhabitants of creatures within each of the 4 main zones. You can click up top on each of the drop down buttons to learn more about each layer, as well as about the interaciton between the animals we have highlighted.
